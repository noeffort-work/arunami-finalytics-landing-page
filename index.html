FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you get started and make the most of our platform.

How does the AI analysis work?

Our AI automatically processes the sales and profit data you input, then gives you clear insights like trends, comparisons, and summaries.

Can I export my reports?

Yes, all your data can be exported to PDF with one click, making it easy to share with your team or management.

How many users can access the platform?

Starter includes 1 user, Standard supports up to 5 users, and Premium offers unlimited users.

Do I need to manually calculate profit and loss?

No. Once you input your numbers, the system automatically calculates profit, loss, and targets for you.

Is my data safe?

Yes, all your uploaded data is stored securely and only accessible to you and your authorized team members.

Can I change my plan later?

Of course! You can upgrade or downgrade your subscription anytime directly from your account settings.
